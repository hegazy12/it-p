Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is an upcoming first-person shooter video game produced by Infinity Word and published and distributed by Activision, and it will be the sixteenth edition of the Call of Duty game series. It will be a reboot of the Modern Warfare sub-chain. The game uses a new engine for this part, allowing the use of more detailed environments. The game is scheduled to be released on October 25, 2019, on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. The game is set in a realistic and modern atmosphere. For the first time in the series' history, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare will support multiplayer on all platforms. Activision also confirmed that the franchise's traditional season passes have been revoked, enabling the company to distribute post-launch content for free to all players.
