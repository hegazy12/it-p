Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War is a first-person shooter video game developed by Treyarch and Raven Software and published by Activision. It is the sixth part in the Black Ops series and the seventeenth in the Call of Duty series. The game takes place after Call of Duty: Black Ops (2010) and before Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 (2012). Black Ops Cold War was released on November 13, 2020. Black Ops Cold War is set in the early 1980s during the Cold War. The single-player story follows CIA officer Russell Adler as he pursues a Soviet spy named "Perseus" with the stated goal of sabotaging the United States and tilting the balance of power in favor of the Soviet Union. Multiplayer mode includes new game modes and more extensive customization options.
