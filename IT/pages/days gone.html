Days Gone is an action-adventure open world zombie horror video game released on April 26, 2019 exclusively on PlayStation 4. The game is developed by SCE Bend Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The game contains an Arabic translation of the lists and dubbing of sounds in the Egyptian dialect, and the Arabic version was released under the title Days Gone. The game was released on PC on May 18, 2021.
