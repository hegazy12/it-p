Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an Action Role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. This is the twelfth major installment and the twenty-second edition in the Assassin's Creed series and the successor to the Assassin's Creed Odyssey game. The game takes place in 873 AD, and narrates the history of the Vikings' invasion of Britain. The player controls Ivor, a Viking attacker who becomes embroiled in the conflict between the Brotherhood of Assassins and the Templars. The game was officially released on November 10, 2020 on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Google Stadia. On PlayStation 5 on November 12, 2020 in North America and Australasia, and released in Europe on November 19, 2020. It is the first game from Ubisoft for the ninth generation of game consoles PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X.
